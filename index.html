I am a creative wearing two hats as a Graphic Designer and Web Developer with an impeccable passion for design and technology. I enjoy using my design and technological intellect to help companies produce meaningful works and communicate compelling stories. I currently reside in the Washington D.C. metropolitan area.

I am first a Designer: My passion for art and creativity inspired me to pursue a Bachelor of Fine Arts with a focus on Graphic Design.Turning ideas into visuals allows me to experience my supterpower. Every design I create is a chnace to inspire, connect and leave a mark that matters.

Over 90% of global communication now involves or passes through computers and digital systems in some form. Realizing this, I decided to learn coding. Coding teaches me how to break down complex problems in manageable steps. This logical, structured thinking is valuable not just in tech, but in everyday decision-making and many other careers.

My approach to design is empathetic and inclusive. Here are some main areas I consider in my approach.

User Centered Design: I always design with my audience in mind. Understanding their needs, perferences, and behavior to create visuals that connect and communicate clearly.

Simplicity with Purpose:I strip away the unnecessary and focus on clean, intentional elements. A simple design with a strong concept always makes a bigger impact.

Consistency and Flexibility: I work hard to maintain visual consistency (fonts, color, layout) to build brand recognition, but stay flexible enough to adapt your design to different formats and trends without losing identity.

Collaboration and Feedback: I believe in the power of collaboration. I actively seek feedback from clients and peers to refine my designs and ensure they align with the project goals.

I am looking for a company that values creativity, innovation, and collaboration. A place where I can contribute my design skills and learn from others. I want to work with a team that is passionate about creating meaningful experiences and pushing the boundaries of design.

I am working toward obtaining acertificate in Front End Development. We now live in a digital world and every visual created get presented to users through coding. Knowing how to code or the technical aspects of how things work is key.
